* Great creative minds think like artists but work like accountants.
* To make most out of deep work, make strict rituals
* If you wanna make it, you gotta push your brain to the limit. (Remember the reason why you wanna do this).

Questions to answer
    - Where you'll work - Avoid distractions, do not disturb
    - For how long you'll work - Make a discrete challenge, not an open ended slog
    - How you'll work once you start to work - this helps you save your willpower reserves
    - How you'll support your work - What do you need to do to be able to keep operating at full power.

* You should be willing to experiment until you find the right ritual for you.
